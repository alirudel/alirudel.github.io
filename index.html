About Me

I'm an art school drop-out, mom of three, pisces and total word nerd. I have a passion for local food and agriculture and supporting small businesses. When I'm not at my desk you can find me in my garden, at my local farmers' market or in the kitchen.

I graduated from The College of William & Mary with a degree in Linguistics and I've been working behind the scenes to build recognizable brands, create content and manage social media for small organizations for over a decade.

In 2020 I enrolled in a Full Stack Development Certificate Program through Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I'm excited to be digging deeper into web development and can't wait to show you what I'm learning!
